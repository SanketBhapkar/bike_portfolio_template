About Us

IRONRIDE is a custom bike modification studio built by riders, for riders. We specialize in performance upgrades, custom builds, and precision detailing that transform machines into personal statements.

Shop Owner

Matt Owen

Matt Owen is the founder and lead builder at IRONRIDE, with years of hands-on experience in motorcycle customization and performance engineering. What began as a personal passion for machines evolved into a full-service custom bike workshop built on skill, precision, and attention to detail.

From concept to final assembly, Matt is closely involved in every build, ensuring each motorcycle meets the highest standards of performance, safety, and craftsmanship. His approach combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of what riders expect from a truly custom machine.
